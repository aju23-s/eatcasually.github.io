🔴 Chicken Alfredo Recall Shakes Grocery Chains

The FDA announced a major recall of pre-packaged chicken fettuccine alfredo meals sold by FreshRealm under the Marketside and Home Chef brands at Walmart and Kroger. The meals are linked to a Listeria outbreak causing 16 hospitalizations and 3 deaths in 13 U.S. states.

🥒 Sonic's “Big Dill Meal” Goes National

Sonic Drive-In is launching an all-pickle lineup with Grillo’s Pickles on June 30, 2025. The “Big Dill Meal” includes:

  • Big Dill Smash Burgers
  • Pickle-Seasoned Fries and Tots

  • Limited-edition pickle-scented car air fresheners

⚠️ New Campaign Targets Ultra-Processed Foods

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services is investing up to $20M in a public campaign warning against the health risks of ultra-processed foods. However, some experts are raising concerns over conflicts of interest involving campaign advisors with ties to tech and food corporations.
